BAT MAN


Batman, created by Bob Kane and Bill Finger, made his debut in 1939 in Detective Comics #27. Beneath the persona of billionaire Bruce Wayne lies Gotham City's masked vigilante, who embarked on a lifelong mission after witnessing his parents' murder. Lacking superpowers, Batman relies on his intellect, physical prowess, and an array of gadgets stored in his secret Batcave, situated beneath Wayne Manor. His trusted allies include Alfred Pennyworth and various sidekicks like Robin and Batgirl. Batman's rogues' gallery boasts iconic villains like the Joker, and he's celebrated as "The World's Greatest Detective" for his exceptional investigative skills. Committed to justice without killing, Batman's adaptability, symbolism, and enduring appeal have made him a beloved cultural icon in comics, movies, and beyond. Batman's symbol, a stylized bat, has become an iconic emblem of justice and fear in Gotham City. It strikes fear into the hearts of criminals and serves as a symbol of hope for the city's residents.Batman has a strict moral code that prohibits killing criminals. He believes in justice and strives to bring criminals to justice through legal means whenever possible.
